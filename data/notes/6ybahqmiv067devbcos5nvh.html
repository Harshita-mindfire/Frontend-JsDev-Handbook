Preposition

word placed before a noun or pronoun to show in what relation the person or thing denoted by it stands in regard of something else.

  • for, from, in, on are often omitted before nouns of place or time. eg: We did it last week. I cannot walk a yard. Wait a minute.

  • no preposition before these words:

    net, last each, every

WHEN

  • at: most specific period of time: hour, minute.
  • on: wider then a specific time: one date, one day
  • in : year, month

WHERE

  • at: specific place. doesn't matter if the name is specified or not. eg: airport, hotel
  • in: city, country
